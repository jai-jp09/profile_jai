About me!

I am final-year undergraduate student from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. I’m pursuing an Integrated MSc. in Economics with a minor in Mathematics and Computing. Along with my college studies, I have cleared the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) Level 1 and Financial Risk Manager (FRM) Part 1.

I’ve been working with Titan Capital for the last 8 months, an early-stage sector agnostic family fund of founders of Snapdeal, with a ticket size of $300k to $500k and a total portfolio of around 300 companies. My daily responsibilities involve interacting with founders to source potential investment opportunities, evaluate companies, conduct in-depth research, analyse financials, and develop investment thesis.

I also worked as a Research Associate at IIM Mumbai (formerly NITIE Mumbai) under the mentorship of Prof. Manoj Tiwari, the current Director of IIM Mumbai. During my 18-month tenure, I contributed to multiple industrial projects in collaboration with leading organizations such as P&G, o9 Solutions, Merck, and Dalmia Cement. My research work received notable recognition, resulting in the publication of two papers in a prestigious journal specializing in optimization and AI/ML.

In addition to my professional journey, I have held key leadership roles as the Chief Editor of Awaaz, IIT Kharagpur's independent media body, and as the General Secretary for Sports. These roles highlight my dedication to fostering leadership, personal growth, and collaboration within diverse ecosystems.

To discuss about Finance, Stock Market, Data science, AI, Application of AI, or potential collaborations, reach out me at jai.iitkgp@gmail.com or +91-7070439778.

If you are building something amazing and looking to raise investments, we are here to back you! Feel free to drop me at jai@titancapital.vc

Looking forward to connecting with you!
